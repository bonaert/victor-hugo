Projet Bordet

Created a website for Brussels' network of university hospitals. The goal of the project is to help medicine students during their residency. When confronted with a pathology or a symptom, they can consult the site to discover links with other pathologies, examples, notes and other information.

Thus, they don't have to bring heavy and impractical dictionaries and can always easily find and access the information that helps them do their job.

Features


  • Central searchable list to quickly find the pathology they're interested in
  • Detailed pathology page showing the links with other pathologies, examples and notes
  • Responsive site that adapts to smartphones, tablets and computers
  • Simple and clear interface, to put the focus on the content and avoid distractions

As wished by the client:


  • Integration with ULB's account system - ULB students can access the service for free using their ULB credentials (no need to create a new account)
  • For others, they can subscribe and pay through credit cards or Bancontact
  • Secure admin mode: the client than add or change the information on the website
  • Automatic backups: the data is preserved even if there's an unexpected problem
