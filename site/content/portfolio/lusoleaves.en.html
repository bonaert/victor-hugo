Lusoleaves

Lusoleaves, an agriculture company, wished to create a website, to accomplish these goals:

  • Attract and inform potentials clients through the Internet
  • Show their offer and make it easy for clients to find and contact them
  • Show the products' current price and availability
  • Be able at all times to check the information from their weather station, to react to frosts and avoid the destruction of the year's production
  • Show the site in English, Portuguese and Spanish, to maximize the reach of the site
